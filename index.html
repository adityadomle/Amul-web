1946

It all began when milk became a symbol of protest

Founded in 1946, Amul started as a cooperative dairy movement, empowering Gujarat's farmers. It evolved into an iconic brand, symbolizing quality, self-reliance, and community strength.

Awards &

Achivements

World Dairy Innovation Awards-2020

FoodBev Dairy Awrads announced World dairy Innovation 2020 on 16th june, 2020 through a virtual Awrad ceremony. Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd. (Amul) is the finalist in the following categories.

IT Awards

GCMMF won “Making of Developed India - IT Awards - For Digital India” in the category of “Leveraging IT for Business Performance” .

Amul receives Green Globe Foundation Award

India's largest food products marketing organization and apex body of dairy cooperatives of Gujarat - has won the "Green Globe Foundation Award" in the Manufacturing Category at the Delhi Sustainable Development Summit 2012 .

IMC Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Trophy-2018

Dr. R.S. Sodhi, Managing Director, received the award during the 22nd IMC Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award function held at IMC Chamber of Commerce & Industry, Churchgate, Mumbai on 15th March, 2019.
